What we aim to do

Do you find yourself punching walls on friday night because you can't calculate the branch current in a RC circuit? Do you bring your calculator to dinner and a movie while ignoring your mates texts? Do you find yourself putting websites about tinkering and building with electronics in an 'incognito tab'? If you answered yes to any of these questions we want to welcome you to your second home.

Designed, created, updated by people like you. EE Sundae is the place to go when you are struggling over a circuit design or analysis homework, solving differential equations, or just want some help with a specific topic.

As you can obviously tell, we are still currently working on the site as we are adjusting to the start of a new semester and learning new material along side people like you. We are not perfect, so if you find any imperfections while browsing what we have created thus far, please do not hesitate to let us know. Stay tuned for video tutorials, conceptual write-ups, and tutoring services for homeworks and exams.
